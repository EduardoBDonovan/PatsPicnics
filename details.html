Details

At Pat's Picnics, we believe that every picnic should be stress-free and enjoyable for our clients.


That's why we provide a full set up and clean up service for every picnic, so all you need to do is show up and relax. Each of our picnics includes the following items to make your experience as comfortable and convenient as possible:


  • Pillows & Rugs: We provide comfortable pillows and rugs to lounge on during your picnic.
  • Umbrella: Our umbrellas provide shade and protection from the sun, ensuring that you can enjoy your picnic in comfort.
  • Plates & Silverware: Our eco-friendly plates and silverware make it easy to enjoy your picnic treats without worrying about disposable items.
  • Wine Glasses: We provide wine glasses so you can toast to your special occasion in style.
  • Bluetooth Speaker: Enjoy your favorite tunes with our Bluetooth speaker, which you can connect to your phone or other devices.
  • Ice Bucket and Waters: Stay hydrated with our complimentary ice bucket and bottles of water.
